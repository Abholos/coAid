Common Safety Advice

Wash your hands often

Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water.

Wear a mask

A mask is required around other people. The appropriate use, cleaning & disposal are essential to make masks as effective.

Keep distance

Maintain at least a 1m distance between yourself and others to reduce the risk of infection. The further away, the better.

Stay at home

Stay at home and self-isolate even if you have minor symptoms such as cough, headache, mild fever, until you recover.

How to use this page



  • This site is for everyone. If you want to find a place, food, first-aid you should come here. If you want to share something you have in surplus with others, you should come here.

  • Once you are at the locations page a map with several pins will appear. You can click those pins to get information about a running event. Also if you want to share you can click the ADD NEW EVENT button and place your own event!

  • After clicking the "ADD NEW EVENT" button a modal window will appear in front of you. Enter title and in the info you can enter any POI and any address you want.Press "ADD EVENT" button and Google maps will post a pin close to your information you provide. Here are some examples:
    1. Full address: Karaiskaki 17, Athens 105 54
    2. POI: Athens International Airport
    3. City or Village: Amnatos

  • You can click the pins to see information for each event.

  • You should check our FAQ to see how to organize your event according to CoVid-19 measures and also contact us if you need further help.

  • Finally, you should check our news feed to be updated all the time about upcoming events and other news.
